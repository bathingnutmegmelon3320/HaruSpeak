## 📖 About this application

HaruSpeak helps you practice speaking Japanese, Chinese, and English. The application uses your computer microphone to listen to your voice. It converts your speech to text and gives you feedback on your pronunciation. You can talk to an artificial intelligence tutor to improve your fluency. The software runs on your machine. Your voice data remains private because everything happens locally. 

## ⚙️ System requirements

Ensure your computer meets these requirements before you begin:

*   Operating System: Windows 10 or Windows 11.
*   Processor: A modern processor with at least 4 cores.
*   Memory: 8 GB of RAM or more.
*   Storage: 2 GB of free disk space.
*   Microphone: A functional headset or built-in computer microphone.
*   Internet Connection: An initial connection to download the application and language models.

## 🧠 Configuring language models

HaruSpeak needs an artificial intelligence engine to understand your speech and provide responses. You can use local software like Ollama or LM Studio to host these engines.

1.  Download and install Ollama or LM Studio from their official websites.
2.  Open your chosen software and search for a model such as Llama 3 or Mistral.
3.  Download the model to your local storage.
4.  Copy the address or port number provided by the software if required.
5.  Open HaruSpeak settings and enter the address where your model runs.
6.  Click Save to apply your changes.

## 🎙️ Practicing your speaking

Once setup is complete, you can start a conversation.

1.  Select your target language from the menu. HaruSpeak supports Japanese, Chinese, and English.
2.  Select a topic from the provided list. 
3.  Click the microphone icon to begin your turn.
4.  Speak clearly into your microphone.
5.  Click the icon again to stop recording.
6.  Wait a moment while the software analyzes your speech.
7.  Read the text on your screen. The software shows furigana for Japanese characters or pinyin for Chinese characters to help with reading.
8.  Listen to the feedback provided by the artificial intelligence.

## 🛠️ Frequently asked questions

**Where is my voice data stored?**
Your voice data stays on your local computer. It does not go to a cloud server.

**Can I use this without an internet connection?**
Yes. After you download the software and the language models, the application works offline. You do not need to be online to practice your speaking. 

**The software does not recognize my voice.**
Check your microphone settings in Windows. Ensure that the correct input device is selected. Speak at a normal pace and volume. Avoid background noise during your practice sessions.

**What is furigana?**
Furigana provides reading guides for Japanese kanji characters. It appears in small text above the kanji to help you identify the pronunciation.

**What is pinyin?**
Pinyin is a method of writing Chinese using the Roman alphabet. It helps you learn the pronunciation of Chinese characters.

**How do I update the software?**
When a new version is available, visit the releases page again. Download the new installer and run it. The new version replaces the old one while keeping your settings.

**Can I change the voice of the tutor?**
Yes. Navigate to the sound menu in the settings tab. You can select different voices for the playback feature. Ensure you have the necessary voice packs downloaded if prompted.

**Why does the software use so much memory?**
The software runs artificial intelligence models locally. These models require significant computer power to generate text and understand speech in real time. Close other programs if your computer runs slowly.
